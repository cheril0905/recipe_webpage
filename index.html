Matar Paneer



Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Green peas
  • Onion
  • Tomato


Process

Saute onions and tomatoes in oil, add spices, then add boiled peas and paneer. Simmer with water for 10 minutes to form a rich gravy.

Gajar Halwa



Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee


Process

Grate carrots and cook in milk till soft. Add sugar and ghee, stir till thickened and aromatic. Garnish with dry fruits.

Dahi Chaat



Ingredients

  • Curd
  • Boiled potato
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sev


Process

Place potatoes in a bowl, top with curd, chutneys, and sev. Sprinkle chaat masala for tangy flavor and serve chilled.
